Our Essence

Fleur Amour is a personalized fragrance discovery platform designed to help you find perfumes that reflect your mood, personality, and lifestyle. Instead of choosing scents by brand or chance, Fleur Amour focuses on how a fragrance makes you feel, guiding you toward scents that resonate on a deeper, more personal level. Through a simple and intuitive experience, users can create a profile, explore scent families, and receive tailored fragrance recommendations. Blending technology with emotion, Fleur Amour transforms fragrance selection into a meaningful, elegant, and luxurious journey—because a perfume is more than a scent, it is an expression of who you are.
